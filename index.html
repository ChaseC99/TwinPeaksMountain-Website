Twin Peaks Mountain

Poway, California

Twin Peaks Mountain is one of the best hikes in Poway, California. After just a short 30 minute, 2 mile hike, you will have gained 700ft in elevation. Sitting at 1,306ft above sea level, the view of San Diego from the top is absolutely spectacular.
